Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking date. The remaining balance is due one week before the scheduled session.

How long until I receive my photos?

Standard delivery time is 2-3 weeks for portrait sessions and 4-6 weeks for weddings and events. Rush editing is available for an additional fee.

What happens if it rains on my outdoor session date?

If weather conditions are unsuitable, we'll reschedule your session at no additional cost. I'll monitor the forecast and communicate with you leading up to your session date.

Do you travel for photo sessions?

Yes, I'm available for travel within a 30-mile radius at no additional cost. Beyond that, a travel fee of $0.58 per mile applies. For destination photography, please contact me for a custom quote.
