About Malcolm

My Photography Journey...

I'm Malcolm Lismore, a professional photographer with over 15 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments. My passion for photography began when I received my first camera as a teenager, and that passion has only grown stronger over the years.

After studying Fine Arts and Photography at the University of Arts, I embarked on a journey to develop my own unique style. I've had the privilege of working with hundreds of clients, from newlyweds to corporate executives, helping them preserve their special moments through my lens.

What sets my work apart is my dedication to understanding each client's vision and bringing it to life. I believe that great photography is about more than just technical skill it's about connecting with people and capturing the emotion and essence of a moment.

When I'm not behind the camera, I enjoy hiking, exploring new landscapes, and spending time with my family. These experiences continually inspire my work and help me bring a fresh perspective to each photography session.

My Specialties

Portrait Photography

Capturing personalities and emotions in a way that tells your unique story. From individual portraits to family sessions, I focus on creating images that reflect who you truly are.

Wedding Photography

Documenting your special day with a blend of candid moments and artistic compositions. I work discreetly to capture the love, joy, and emotion that make your wedding day unique.

Landscape Photography

Showcasing the beauty of nature through carefully composed and thoughtfully processed images. My landscape work aims to transport viewers to the moment when the image was captured.

Event Coverage

Comprehensive photography for corporate events, conferences, and special occasions. I ensure no important moment is missed while maintaining a professional and unobtrusive presence.

Commercial Photography

Creating compelling images for businesses that help showcase products and services in their best light. I work closely with clients to understand their brand and marketing objectives.

Fine Art Photography

Artistic expressions that go beyond documentation to create images with emotional impact. My fine art work has been exhibited in galleries and is available for purchase as prints.
